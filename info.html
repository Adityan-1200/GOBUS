What is FlexiTicket
The FlexiTicket is a flexible bus ticketing option designed to provide travelers with greater convenience and peace of mind. This feature allows for free rescheduling and partial refunds, making it ideal for those who may need to adjust their travel plans.

Key Benefits of FlexiTicket

1.Free Date Change :- You can modify your travel date at no additional cost if the change is made at least 8 hours before

2.Partial Refund on Cancellation If you cancel your ticket 12 hours or more before departure, you are eligible for a minimum 50% refund.

3.Eligibility for Offers Travelers booking FlexiTrips are eligible for ongoing offers and cashbacks, enhancing the value of their purchase.

What is Assurance Program
Assurance Program is an optional add-on designed to provide travelers with enhanced security and peace of mind during their bus journeys. By opting for this program, passengers can avail themselves of additional benefits that safeguard against unforeseen circumstances.

Key Benefits of Assurance Program

1.Protection Against Bus Operator Cancellations

2.Extra Refund for Cancellations

3.Accidental Medical Coverage

What is Bus Timetable
Timetable is an essential feature for travelers seeking to plan their bus journeys effectively. It provides comprehensive information on bus departures, arrivals, and routes, helping passengers make informed decisions about their travel schedules.

Key Features of the redBus Timetable

1.Comprehensive Coverage The timetable includes data for various bus operators across multiple routes, allowing travelers to compare options and choose the most convenient schedules.

2.Real-Time Updates Passengers can access up-to-date information on bus timings, ensuring they have the latest details for their planned journeys.

3.Detailed Route InformationThe timetable provides insights into the routes taken by buses, including stops and estimated travel times, aiding in better trip planning.
